Text Alignment

In (human) languages start-aligned body text is always preferred over justified text because the ragged end edge helps the eye find the next line.

by Charles Iliya Krempeaux (reiver)

Text Alignment affects Text Readability. There are 4 common types of text alignment:

  • left
  • right
  • center
  • justify
